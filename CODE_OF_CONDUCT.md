# Code of Conduct

Pinpoint is an open technical community maintained by CodePal. This policy applies to the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, Discussions, project events, and anyone representing the project in another public space.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence without attacking the person presenting them.
- Give concrete, actionable feedback and accept correction when evidence changes.
- Welcome contributors regardless of experience, identity, background, or affiliation.
- Respect privacy. Never post credentials, private prompts, raw agent traces, or another person's identifying information.
- Disclose relevant conflicts of interest when reviewing benchmarks, integrations, or competing products.
- Keep project spaces free of harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, spam, and sustained disruption.

Technical disagreement is normal. Hostility, intimidation, and bad-faith misrepresentation are not.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to [support@codepal.ai](mailto:support@codepal.ai) with the subject `Pinpoint conduct report`. Include links, dates, and screenshots when useful, but do not send credentials or private prompt content. Security vulnerabilities follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) instead.

CodePal will acknowledge a conduct report within seven days, limit details to people handling the report, and avoid assigning a report to someone directly involved in it. Retaliation against a reporter or participant in an investigation is prohibited.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit content, close interactions, issue a private or public warning, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Decisions consider severity, impact, repeated behavior, acknowledgement, and repair. Illegal threats or immediate safety risks may be referred to the relevant service provider or authorities.

Questions about this policy may be raised in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/CodePalAI/pinpoint/discussions) or privately through the reporting address above.
